Instructions

The initial screening exam to select Sri Lankan team for International Olympiad in Informatics IOI 2017 will be held on Jan 25, 2017 from 3:00PM to 3:30PM (30 minutes) as an online multiple choice question(MCQ) test.

Students screened from this online test will be selected for further training on computer programming. The training will be conducted by Dr. Harshana Dantanarayana from Loughborough University, UK a former IOI medal winner.

After this training on basic computer programming there will be another screening test at UCSC, University of Colombo to select best students for the Sri Lankan IOI pool. The Sri Lankan IOI pool will receive a training on advanced data structures and algorithms. Finally, 4 student from that will be selected to represent Sri Lanka at International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) 2017.

Who can participate

School students who are over 12 years and under 20 years of age (by 1st July, 2017) can participate.

Nature of the questions:

The question will be set to measure students analytical and reasoning skills. So the test will be more like an IQ test.

Scoring method

Number of points allocated for each question is given in the question description. If you correctly answer the question you get the number of points mentioned. If you answer incorrectly you get negative points. Number of negative points is equal to 1/4th of the points allocated to the question. (e.g. if you answer incorrectly to a question worth 20 points, you’ll get -20 / 4 = -5 points). If no answer is given, you'll get 0 point for that question.

Date/Duration

Jan 25, 2017 3:00PM - 3:30PM Sri Lankan time. (30 minutes).

Infrastructure Requirements

You need to have access to a computer with a modern web browser (i.e. Google Chrome, Internet Explorer, Firefox etc) and internet connectivity during above mentioned period.

Submission

Please make sure to submit by clicking Submit button before 3:30PM, otherwise none of your responses will be recorded. You can change your answer by clicking 'Edit your responses’ after clicking Submit button.

Medium

English (Question will be phrased in Sinhala and Tamil)
